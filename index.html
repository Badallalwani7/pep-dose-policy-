PepDose Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

PepDose ("the App") is developed by Badal Lalwani, an independent developer ("I," "me," "my"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information I Collect

The App is designed to store most of your data locally on your device. If you choose to sign in, I collect and process the following:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, I receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google, used solely to identify your account.
  • Protocol and health tracking data: Information you manually enter, including peptide/compound names, dosages, injection logs, inventory details, and health metrics (such as weight, sleep, mood, and related wellness data).
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g. which features are used, app open frequency) via Firebase Analytics, to help me improve the App.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: Technical information about app crashes or errors via Firebase Crashlytics, used solely for fixing bugs.
  • Subscription information: If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed entirely by Google Play. I do not receive or store your payment card details. I receive only subscription status (active/inactive, tier) via RevenueCat.

2. How I Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's core features
  • To back up your data to the cloud (Firebase/Firestore), if you are signed in and have an Elite subscription, so you can restore your data on a new device
  • To understand how the App is used, in order to improve it
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your protocol, injection, inventory, and health metric data is stored locally on your device by default. If you sign in and have an eligible subscription, this data may also be securely synced to Google Firebase servers, accessible only to your authenticated account. I use industry-standard security practices including authenticated, user-isolated cloud storage rules.

4. Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal information. I do not share your health or protocol data with third parties for advertising purposes. I use the following third-party service providers, each of which processes data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Google Play Billing (payment processing)

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can use the App's core features without ever signing in or creating an account.
  • You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data.
  • You can sign out at any time; your local data remains on your device.
  • To request deletion of cloud-stored data associated with your account, contact me using the details below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. I do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Medical Disclaimer

PepDose is a tracking and organizational tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding any substance use, dosage, or health decision.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact
